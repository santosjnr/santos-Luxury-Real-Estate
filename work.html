Buying & Selling with Confidence

Your step-by-step guide to exploring, purchasing, and listing luxury real estate using Web3 tools and secure smart contracts.

1. Explore Verified Listings

Browse high-end properties worldwide. Filter by region, price, and asset type (villa, penthouse, private island).

2. Connect Your Wallet

Securely connect your crypto wallet (e.g., MetaMask). No signups or passwords required.

3. Buy via Smart Contract

Each listing is tokenized as an NFT. You purchase property ownership on-chain using ETH or stablecoins.

4. Ownership Recorded on Blockchain

Your ownership is secured and visible on the Ethereum blockchain — tamper-proof and verifiable.

5. List Your Property

Own a luxury property? Tokenize and list it for sale or fractional ownership directly from your wallet.
